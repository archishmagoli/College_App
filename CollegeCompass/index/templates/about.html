About CollegeCompass

CollegeCompass is a web platform that helps prospective college students navigate the college admissions process more smoothly.

Some features of the platform include: a news page, a financial aid section, and a mental health corner. It also provides other much-needed tools, such as a checklist, a colleges page, and a contacts page for students to record information about their college mentors.

This platform was created by a group of students for the MetroHacks EmpowHer CS Competition.
